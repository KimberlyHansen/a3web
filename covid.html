Covid Travel Restrictions in Australia

The Australian border are currently closed and the only people who can travel to Australia are:

Australian Citizens

Residents

Immediate Family Members

Travellers who have been in New Zealand for the pervious 14 days

Information fo Incoming Travellers

Travellers will receive health screening once they arrive in Austalia to help reduce the spread of COVID 19

Travellers must quarantine for 14 days once they have arrived into the Country and after the 14 days travellers you can start traveling within the country

Advice for Travellers

Prepare to quarentine by bringing the things you need for 2 weeks. These 2 weeks give you a lot of time to plan out your trip

Be aware of the COVID 19 symptoms in case you do get the virus. Some symptoms include fever, cough and loss of taste or smell

Wear a mask when needed

Wash your hands frequently

Social distance by staying 6 feet awate from other people
