Covid Restrictions in Australia

The Australian border are currently closed and the only people who can travel to Austilia are:

  • Australian Citizen
  • Resident
  • Immediate Family

Incoming Travellers

Travellers will receive health screening once they arrive in Austalia to help reduce the spread of COVID 19

Travellers must quarantine for 14 days once they have arrived into the Country
